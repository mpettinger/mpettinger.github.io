Promptless Few Shot Learning

  • Model Anomaly Detection: Identified and documented critical anomalies within embedding models, leading to a deeper understanding of vector space irregularities and improved model selection.
  • Cross-Functional Collaboration: Partnered with Data Scientists to architect a high-efficiency few-shot learning method that operated independently of traditional LLM prompting, reducing computational overhead and latency.
  • Algorithmic Distribution: Engineered a deployment strategy to make complex ML algorithms accessible and distributable across the broader analyst team, fostering a culture of technical self-sufficiency.
  • Performance Benchmarking: Achieved a significant 70% accuracy rate using only 20 labels per class, demonstrating high-performance classification in data-scarce environments.
  • Iterative Research & Prototyping: Utilized Jupyter Lab to conduct extensive experiments with fine-tuning and labeling techniques. Provided a continuous feedback loop to the data science team that directly informed algorithmic refinements and the discovery of embedding model biases.
